Design-build Service

A design-build service is a project delivery method where the design and construction phases are contracted by a single entity, often a design-build firm. This approach streamlines the process, fosters collaboration, and can result in cost and time savings compared to traditional methods where the design and construction are separate contracts.

  • Single Point of Responsibility
  • Sreamlined Process
  • Cost And Time Saving

Renovation And Restoration

Key differences between renovation and restoration include their objectives, scope, and approach to preserving or changing the original character of a building. While renovation seeks to update and improve existing structures, restoration aims to conserve and honor their historical and architectural heritage.
